Environmental Effects

Plastic pollution is affecting the environment. There has been of plastic in the world we live in now. A main cause of plastic pollution is when enough plastic has gathered in an area that affects the natural environment and harms plants, animals, or humans. Plastic pollution is very harmful to the environment because plastic has toxic pollutants that damage the environment which causes water, land and air pollution. Since the plastic take years for it to break down so the damage to the environment is long lasting.
